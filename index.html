About

My name is Nicko Guyer. I live in Raleigh, NC and I love taking photos to document life, and capture moments that will be treasured for years to come. I enjoy working in many types of photography from landscapes, to newborn portraits, fall family photos, large group events, and engagement photo shoots.

I like to keep things simple and straightforward. I will give you full resolution, digital copies of all the photos I take for you. You have permission to print them, upload them, and share them wherever and whenever you want. Your pictures belong to you!

I have years of experience photographing families, babies, and children of all ages. I would love to help document your or your loved one's next big milestone.
